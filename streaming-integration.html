Distribution & Streaming Integration for Musicians

Direct-to-Platform Uploads:

  • One-Stop Distribution: Upload tracks once and push them to Spotify, Apple Music, Deezer, and more.
  • Metadata Management: Easily add titles, album art, and credits to ensure your music appears professionally.
  • Global Reach: Make your music accessible to fans worldwide, expanding your audience beyond local scenes.

Performance Monitoring & Analytics:

  • Real-Time Stats: Track play counts, playlist placements, and listener demographics as they evolve.
  • Identify Popular Tracks: Pinpoint which songs perform best to guide setlists and promotional campaigns.
  • User Retention Data: Understand skip rates, listener retention, and completion percentages to refine your sound.

Revenue & Royalties:

  • Automated Royalty Tracking: Ensure you receive accurate payouts from streaming services.
  • Sync Licensing Opportunities: Explore placements in film, TV, and ads to diversify your income streams.
  • Release Scheduling: Plan strategic release dates for maximum impact, such as dropping singles before tours.

Suggestions & Tips:

  • Optimize Your Profile: Update bios, images, and merch links on streaming platforms to engage fans.
  • Curate Playlists: Create playlists featuring your influences and similar artists to enhance discovery.
  • Promote New Releases: Use social media, mailing lists, and pre-save campaigns to build hype before launch.
  • Test Release Strategies: Experiment with singles, EPs, and full albums to see what format resonates most.
  • Keep Your Catalog Fresh: Remaster older tracks, release live sessions, or offer acoustic versions for loyal listeners.
