About Craig Adams

With over 12 years of experience specializing in luxury real estate photography, I bring a unique artistic vision and technical expertise to every property I photograph.

Based in Melbourne, I've worked with the city's top real estate agencies and developers, helping them showcase their premium listings in the best possible light. My work has been featured in prestigious publications like Domain, The Age Property, and Luxury List.

What sets me apart is my ability to understand the unique selling points of each property and create images that not only document but elevate the space, telling a compelling visual story that resonates with high-end buyers.
