  • Automated Reminders: Receive alerts about upcoming gigs, deadlines for contracts, and payment due dates.

Booking Requests & Negotiations:

  • Direct Communication: Connect with venue managers, festival organizers, or private event hosts without intermediaries.
  • Built-In Contract Templates: Quickly draft agreements to clarify payment, performance length, and equipment needs.
  • Data-Driven Negotiations: Reference past gig fees, attendance figures, and market data to secure fair compensation.

Secure & Automated Payments:

  • Instant Deposits: Collect partial or full payments upfront to guarantee income and reduce last-minute cancellations.
  • Online Invoicing: Send professional, itemized invoices that streamline accounting and tax reporting.
  • Dispute Resolution: Access support for resolving payment issues or contract disagreements, providing peace of mind.

Suggestions & Tips:

  • Maintain Professionalism: Respond to booking inquiries promptly and courteously to build trust with venues.
  • Set Clear Expectations: Define set lengths, tech requirements, and payment terms upfront to avoid misunderstandings.
  • Track Performance History: Keep records of past gigs, attendance, and audience feedback to justify your rates.
  • Leverage Analytics: Use booking data to identify cities and venues where you perform well and plan targeted tours.
  • Stay Organized: Regularly update your calendar and promptly mark shows as confirmed or canceled to avoid scheduling conflicts.
