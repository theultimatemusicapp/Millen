About Us

MusicRevolution was founded by two passionate individuals, Reuben Hester, a British-born artist, and Carl Tomich, a documentary filmmaker and DJ known for the film "Busking for Berlin". Both Reuben and Carl have extensive experience in various facets of the music industry—from performing in bands and DJing to creating music videos and being signed to labels. Witnessing firsthand the struggles that artists face, they joined forces to create a platform aimed at revitalizing a dying industry by empowering musicians and venues alike.
