About Us

MusicRevolution was founded by two passionate individuals, Reuben Hester, a British-born artist, and Carl Tomich, a documentary filmmaker and DJ known for the film "Busking for Berlin". Both Reuben and Carl have extensive experience in various facets of the music industryâ€”from performing in bands and DJing to creating music videos and being signed to labels.
