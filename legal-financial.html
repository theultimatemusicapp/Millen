Secure Payments & Financial Tools

  • Reliable Income Tracking: Ensure timely, accurate compensation for gigs and sales.
  • Safe & Secure Transactions: Protect yourself and buyers from fraud.
  • Automated Invoicing & Reporting: Handle taxes and budgeting easily.

Publishing Rights Management

  • Intellectual Property Protection: Track songwriting credits and protect compositions.
  • Maximize Royalty Earnings: Receive proper royalties from streaming and radio.
  • Licensing & Synchronization: Explore TV, film, and ad opportunities.

Suggestions & Tips

  • Seek Professional Guidance: Consult a lawyer or advisor if needed.
  • Stay Organized: Keep records of contracts, invoices, and royalty statements.
  • Review & Update Regularly: Revisit agreements as your career evolves.
  • Protect Your Rights Internationally: Consider global registration.
  • Leverage Technology: Use digital tools for automated royalty collection and reporting.
